Top three activities to do at Nairobi, Kenya

Nairobi National Park

The Nairobi National Park is expansive and offers sightings of variety of wild animals.

Ngong Hills

The Ngong Hills is perfect for picnic, hiking, and ziplining while watching the fantastic views of the Great Rift Valley.

Railway Museum

The Nairobi Museum is rich in art collection and consists of relics from the East African Railway.
